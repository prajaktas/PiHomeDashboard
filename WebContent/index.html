Get Access to Your Data and Save Energy !
Do it with just a click on Think Green

JustSmartThings have provided a 'Think Green' button which will allow the utility customers to view and save their PG&E energy usage details. These details will be seen in the form of a dashboard, a dashboard consisting of pie-charts, bar graphs and other forms of data visualization. By viewing this data the consumer can manage their energy usage and save on their bills. The Think green button is not limited to the monthly bills but it gives a detailed information about energy consumption.
